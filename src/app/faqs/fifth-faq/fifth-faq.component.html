How do I register for Competitions at Mood Indigo?

If you do not have an MI Number, go to the 'Register' tab and fill in your details there to get an MI Number. Then go to Competitions > Genre and register in the required competition. If you already have an MI Number then directly register for the competition by going to Competitions > Genre and register in the required competition.


Who can participate in these competitions? Is there any fee involved for registration?

Any college student can take part in these competitions. There is no registration fee for participation.


Are on-spot registrations allowed during the fest?

On-spot registrations may or may not be allowed depending on the number of already registered participants. We suggest that you register online as soon as possible to prevent any unforeseen circumstances.


Can I form a team with students from other colleges?

This depends on the competition you are participating in. Visit individual competitions at www.moodi.org/competitions for details.


What is an LYP?

LYP stands for Live Your Passion. This is an initiative taken up by Mood Indigo in order to promote and showcase the talent of winners of competitions. Wherever applicable, they have been mentioned in the competition details itself under the Prizes & LYP tab.


Will I get a certificate for participation in competitions during Mood Indigo?

Winners and finalists of all the competitions shall get an online certificate after Mood Indigo.


Am I supposed to register for the group competition myself?

If you are the team leader, you must go to the competition you are interested in and create a team and then add the MI Numbers of all your team members. If you are NOT the team leader, ask the team leader to add your MI Number in the team


What is the date, time and venue for these competitions?

These competitions will either be held online or during Mood Indigo depending on the competition. The detailed schedule will be released later.
